Title: Parent's Recipies
Content: ***Dad's Recipe for Spaghetti***
* Some Meat
* 1 Onion
* 1 Pack of Mushrooms (White)
* 5 Tomatoes (Large)

**Directions**
* Cook meat and onions (no need for oil)
* Add oyster sauce and hoisin sauce (about a spoonful)
* Add sugar (1-2 spoonfuls) and cooking wine. No salt needed.
* Add tomatoes and ketchup (1/4 cup full)
* Add pasta sauce
* Mix around
* Add mushrooms
* Cook around 40 minutes
* Maintain rolling boil.
